Long time ago in a galaxy far far away...

Emperor Palpatine executed Order 66 which wiped out all the Jedi. A new era of Sith began. The Republic fell allowing Empire to take over the galaxy.

The Empire ruled by Sith Lord Palpatine and Darth Vader continued to rule galaxy over decades.

Then a new hope arised. A young farm boy Luke Skywalker felt the force calling him. He seek adventure.
